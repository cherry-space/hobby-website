Durian

Durian is a tropical fruit known for its strong smell.
However, it is often referred to as the "king of fruits" because of its taste.
As a person who loves trying new foods, I had to try it!
It was definitely an interesting experience and I really liked its taste.
While it smells terrible, I found that the taste was quite unique and delicious.
It is a must-try for those who are curious!
Frankly speaking, my mom and dad did not like it at all, lol.
